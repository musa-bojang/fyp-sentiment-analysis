Abstract

The emergence of online marketplaces has contributed to the increase in the number of consumers for a product. It also provides an avenue for consumers to give instant feedback from the purchased product. In as much as good feedback helps to attract more sales, a bad consumer experience can affect sales as well. In other words, analysis of consumer sentiment with quickly appropriate response helps to save the business of the day. In this report, a study on sentiment analysis is conducted to assess the opinion of consumers regarding electronic gadgets that are considered niche products for online marketplaces. By exploring machine learning algorithms, the algorithm that gives more accurate result is chosen. The trained model is then used to analyze real-time reviews and comments about these products. The system works by accepting input text from the user, performs sentiment analysis operations, and give results. The system is based on a sentiment analysis algorithm to quantify positive and negative feelings concerning iPhone and android devices.
